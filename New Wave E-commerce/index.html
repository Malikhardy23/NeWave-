About NeWave

NeWave is a lifestyle retailer dedicated to inspiring customers through a unique combination of product, creativity and cultural understanding. Founded in 1990 in a small space across the street from the University at Albany, NeWave now operates over 500 stores in the United States, Canada and Europe, offering experiential retail environments and a well-curated mix of on-trend women’s and men’s clothes, from boho dresses, denim and graphics to shoes, hats, and backpacks, as well as beauty, intimates, swim and a collection of handpicked vintage clothing. NeWave also carries everything you need to decorate your small space, apartment or dorm, with a NW Home collection that includes bedding, tapestries, rugs, shower curtains and furniture. Our Music + Tech collection is a go-to destination for vinyl, record players and tech accessories. Visit our stores, where all are welcome and there’s always something new to see, or shop online at NeWave.com and get free shipping on US orders over $50. For special offers, one-of-a-kind prizes and more, download the NW app and join NW Rewards! Want to be part of the conversation? Follow, share and shop @NeWave.
